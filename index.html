Rules of the Game

Welcome to Chick-Quack-Crow(🐤🦆🐦), a new version of TicTacToe.

In the game, you will take turns against the computer to try and get your three chicks in a row - either horizontally, vertically or diagonally! Try to play strategically by blocking your oponent from getting their three ducks in a row.

Have fun battling for chick supremacy! Good luck!
